BOOMERANG

Toronto's very own lost and found system

Welcome to Boomerang. Who are we?, you may ask. We are members of your community dedicated to help you find items and/or pets that you may have lost. We know certain things and your pets can have a lot of meaning for you and how upsetting it can be to lose them. We are the very first lost and found system in Toronto. If you have lost something or found something that you may think someone may be looking for, simply post an ad and you will find what you're looking for in no time.
